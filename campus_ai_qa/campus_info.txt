Macao Polytechnic University (MPU) Official Campus Information
1. Campus & Location
MPU has two campuses: Main Campus (Macao Peninsula) and Taipa Campus.
Main Campus Address: Rua de Luís Gonzaga Gomes, No. 30, Macao Peninsula (adjacent to Macau Grand Prix Museum and Golden Lotus Square).
Taipa Campus Address: Located in Taipa, convenient for students living in Taipa and Coloane.
Both campuses are easily accessible by public transport and within walking distance to city centers.

4. Campus Facilities
Sports Facilities: Multi-sports Pavilion (over 6,200 sqm, 3,800 seating capacity), indoor sports halls, fitness rooms, and sports courts.
Dining: Three student canteens on campus, providing affordable and nutritious meals.
Student Dormitories: Available on Main Campus, with shared kitchens and common areas on each floor.
Academic Facilities: Modern classrooms, computer labs, specialized labs (nursing, AI, design), lecture halls (Anfiteatro 1: 187 seats, Anfiteatro 2: 96 seats), and a 13-storey new academic building with performance hall and international exam center.
Other Facilities: Campus bookstore, study shared areas, lockers, first-aid boxes, and lost & found service.
5. Student Services
Student Affairs Office: Responsible for student activities, exchange programs, career guidance, scholarships, counseling, and student discipline.
Counseling Service: Free psychological counseling for students, contact: (853) 8599 6141 / 6139 / 6573.
Career Guidance: Job placement support, internship opportunities, and career workshops.
Student Information Platform (SIWeb): One-stop platform for course registration, grade inquiry, service applications, and e-portfolio.
Student Insurance: Mandatory student insurance for all enrolled students.
Venue Booking: Students can book campus venues (classrooms, sports facilities) through the intranet system.

7. General University Information
Founded in 1981, officially renamed Macao Polytechnic University on March 1, 2022.
Public university in Macao, with a graduate employment rate of over 90%.
MPU Motto: "Knowledge, Expertise, Global Vision"
Campus design prioritizes greening and pedestrian-friendly spaces.
For the latest updates, students should check official student emails and the MPU website (www.mpu.edu.mo).
